ENERGY IN AUSTRALIA

An overview of Australia's Energy Export, Generation, Supply and Future

Global Net Energy Exports in 2020

In 2020, Australia had the 3rd largest net export in energy (exports minus imports) of 13,200 Petajoules, after Russia (28,800 PJ) and Saudi Arabia (16,200 PJ).

Main energy types include coal and natural gas, exported primarily to China, Japan, South Korea and India. Australia's net export equates to over 2/3 of primary energy production.

Australia's Generated Energy over the Years

Rich in non-renewable energy resources, Australia has vast reserves throughout the country. Federal and state policies have been favouring the reduction of coal usage and promoting natural gas, a cleaner alternative, producing fewer carbon emissions when burned for electricity generation.
